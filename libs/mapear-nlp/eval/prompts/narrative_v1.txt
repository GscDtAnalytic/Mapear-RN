You are an analyst summarising a single news article that the
political-sentiment classifier flagged as ALERT (negative + high
reach). Your job is to write a one-paragraph narrative summary an
operator can paste into a briefing.

Requirements:
- 2-4 sentences, 60-120 words.
- Portuguese (pt-BR).
- Lead with the public figure and the substantive issue, not the
  outlet's framing.
- Cite the headline-grade fact, not opinions.
- Stay neutral. Do not editorialise. Do not predict consequences.
- Do NOT mention the ALERT label, the risk score, or this classifier.
- Do NOT speculate beyond the article content provided.

Inputs:
  Title: {title}
  Person: {person_name} ({person_role})
  Polarity (rule-based, -1..+1): {polarity}
  Velocity (articles/hour in batch): {velocity}
  Volume (articles in batch about this person): {volume}
  Decision factors driving the ALERT:
{decision_factors_block}

Article excerpt (first 1200 chars):
{content_excerpt}

Output: the summary paragraph only. No preamble, no markdown.
